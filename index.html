How Rapport Report Works

What's a Klout Score

  • Klout Score is an overall index of your social media presence
  • Klout Scores of around 40 are considered average while scores above 65 are in the top 5% of users
  • Klout Scores are generally higher for users active in multiple social media outlets, and who have many followers and shared content
  • To find this data, simply Enter a Twitter handle such as @darthVader or darthVader
  • For more information on Klout Scores, check out Klout.com
